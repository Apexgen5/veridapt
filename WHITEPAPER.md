## Executive Summary

Veridapt is the **decentralized Compliance Oracle Network** — the missing regulatory and identity layer for tokenized real-world assets (RWAs), stablecoins, permissioned DeFi, and institutional onchain finance.

By combining **Zero-Knowledge proofs**, **portable Verifiable Credentials**, **Supra L1's native oracles & automation**, and **cross-chain portability**, Veridapt enables users and institutions to prove regulatory compliance (KYC, AML, age, accreditation, sanctions status, jurisdiction) without revealing personal data.

**We solve the last-mile regulatory bottleneck** that currently prevents tokenized finance from reaching trillions in scale.

## 1. The Problem

- Repeated KYC/AML processes across platforms
- Privacy vs. regulatory transparency conflict on public blockchains
- Fragmented rules across 180+ jurisdictions
- Lack of portable, cryptographically enforceable attestations
- High liability and operational friction for institutions
- Result: RWAs remain <1% of their potential market ($10T–$30T by 2030–2034)

Current solutions are either fully centralized (high risk, single point of failure) or incomplete (partial ZK pilots without full enforcement and portability).

## 2. The Veridapt Solution – Global Compliance Fabric

Veridapt is a hybrid decentralized network that delivers:

- **ZK-KYC & Selective Disclosure**: Prove compliance predicates without exposing PII.
- **Portable Credentials**: One attestation works across any chain (public or permissioned).
- **Automated Enforcement**: Smart contracts automatically allow/block transactions based on compliance status.
- **Oracle Fusion**: Real-time sanctions, risk scores, and dynamic rules via Supra oracles.
- **Hybrid Delivery**: Permissionless protocol + enterprise-grade SaaS APIs and dashboards.

### Key Innovations
- Native integration with **Supra L1** (500k+ TPS, sub-second finality, Moonshot consensus)
- Partnership-ready with **Zyphe** for production-grade issuer network
- Threshold encryption for regulated "break-glass" access
- Compliance Score & Reputation system (onchain, privacy-preserving)

## 3. Architecture

**Core Layers:**
1. **Issuer Network** – Regulated Guardians & Zyphe nodes issue zkCerts
2. **ZK Proof Layer** – Noir circuits for predicates (age, KYC, sanctions, accreditation, jurisdiction)
3. **Verification & Enforcement Engine** – Supra smart contracts + native automation
4. **Oracle & Data Layer** – Supra oracles + enriched onchain data
5. **Cross-Chain Portability** – SupraNova + standards alignment
6. **Governance & Incentives** – Native token + staking

## 4. Tokenomics (VERI)

- **Utility**: Pay for attestations, staking for network security, governance
- **Value Accrual**: Protocol fees (from usage + enterprise) partially burned or distributed to stakers
- **Supply**: Fixed or mildly inflationary with strong utility sink

## 5. Revenue Model
